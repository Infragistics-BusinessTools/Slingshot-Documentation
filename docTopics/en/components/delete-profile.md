## Deleting Profile Data from Slingshot

To meet global data privacy laws, including General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Slingshot gives the users the ability to have all their profile information deleted. Below, you will find who can delete profile data and how, what information will be deleted and what will stay. 

### What is Profile Information? 

In collaboration software like Slingshot, what you do affects the people you work with in this space. If, for example, you start a discussion in a team, this information will be stored in Slingshot to help others. This way, everyone in the team sees that you are the author of the discussion. 

Slingshot considers the following your profile information: 

- your email address;
- all data you shared in the *Profile Information* menu - your name, title, photo, industry and departmant (see more in the [Completing Your Profile Information](profile-information.md)topic);
- all content created by you or shared with you in *Home*;

Deletion is permanent - once deleted, your information can't be recovered in Slingshot. 

<a name="data-not-deleted"></a>
### What Information Will Not Be Deleted?

While collaborating with others in Slingshot, you create content that is important for your shared projects and is not part of your profile information. This content will stay even after your profile information is deleted. The following is not considered profile information by Slingshot: 

- files or dashboards uploaded by the you; 
- teams, projects, tasks or *Analytics* content created by you;
- your messages' content in discussions or private/group chats;
- the content of any @mentions directed at you. 

>[!NOTE] When your profile data is deleted, your name will be displayed as *@deactivateduser* in all messages in discussions, private chats and @mentions. *@deactivateduser* will also be displayed in place of your name in all members lists of teams you didn't leave before deleting your profile. 

### Who Can Delete Profile Data?

Technically speaking, the Slingshot team is the one having rights to delete profile data from Slingshot. But they do this at the request of users, of course. Then, who can request directly profile data deletion from the Slingshot team? External users and Organization managers can contact the Slingshot team and make a data deletion request.  

What if you are part of an Organization? Then the information in your profile is considered ownership of the Organization. To learn how you can have it deleted, read below. 

### How to Delete Profile Data?

#### For Organization Members

So, as mentioned above, if you belong to an Organization, then your data in Slingshot also belongs to the Organization. However, data protection laws specify the cases when you can have all your data deleted by the Organization. Slingshot will help your Organization honor your rights. 

Just contact a manager in your Organization and request deletion of your data. They will continue the process of deletion by contacting the Slingshot team. 

*Option 2: Contact us if you are unable to get in touch with a manager of your Organization and we can help with forwarding your request to your Organization? Can we do this to help users? Is there another alternative process?*

#### For Organization Managers/Leaders 

If you are a manager in charge of personal data in your Organization, and a member has contacted you with a request to delete their profile data from Slingshot, then read the steps below to learn how to proceed.

1. Contact our team at support@slingshot.io or feedback@slingshot.io ?
2. Note you want to delete a member's profile information.
3. Specify the member's email address and your Organization name??? 
4. Deletion may take up to 24 hours. When profile data is finally deleted, the user's name will be displayed as @deactivateduser whereever their content has not disappeared (see [above](#data-not-deleted)).  

#### For External Users

If you are an external user, you do not belong to an Organization. You can be part of Organization's teams and projects but only if invited. In this sense, your profile information do not belong to an Organization and you can request data deletion from the Slingshot team directly. To do this:  

select your profile image > *Settings* > *Data Privacy* > *Delete my Data* (as shown in the screenshot below)

<img src="images/delete-profile-data-settings.png" alt="Deleting your profile data from the Settings menu" witdth="100%">

The deletion process may take up to 24 hours. During that time you will not be able to log into your Slingshot profile.

[!NOTE] If you have been invited to be an external member in an Organization team in Slingshot, you will note lose the right to delete your profile data. However, Organization related data will not be deleted together with your profile information as it is owned by the Organization.

### Can I Reactivate My Profile?

If you are an external user, you can re-activate your profile by simply logging in again after the deletion process has been finished. However, the information you deleted from your profile cannot be recovered. Your history will not be available as well. 

If you are an Organization member, re-activating your profile is your Organization's concern. However, once re-activated your profile will start with clear history.
